NoQ Store

Redefining Retail Through Seamless Experiences

Research & Analysis Information Architecture UX/UI Design
Tools Used Figma | Miro | UserTesting | Jira
Time Line 8 Weeks

NoQStore, a 24x7 digitized UnManned Convenience Store for groceries and FMCG products, sought to revolutionize retail with a self-service, human-free shopping experience. - replace for NoQ description

The Challenge

NoQ’s vision was bold—but the existing mobile experience fell short. Customers faced confusing onboarding and store entry, cluttered interfaces, unclear product navigation, and a multi-step checkout flow that led to cart abandonment. The app needed more than a visual refresh; it required a complete UX overhaul, aligned with the physical store design and user behavior.

The Solution

Empathy-Driven In-Store Audit:

We designed not just for screens—but for real people in physical stores. We conducted usability audits of the legacy app, ran stakeholder interviews, and performed deep user journey analysis to map store entry, scan-to-cart actions, and final payments.

Clear Visual Architecture:

We created a clean, modern interface focusing on minimalism and logical flows. Product pages, cart views, and checkout details were re-architected to minimize cognitive load and eliminate purchasing hesitation.

Seamless Checkout Integration:

To prevent cart abandonment, we streamlined the checkout experience into a secure, one-tap process using Google Pay, digital wallets, and credit cards, making transaction success effortless.

Key Features

We focused on a minimalist, clutter-free interface for the retail mobile app. Our approach prioritized simplicity, ensuring a smooth and intuitive user experience. We crafted a logical flow that guides users effortlessly from entry to checkout.

Smart Onboarding

Geofenced prompts guide new users automatically upon entering the store.

Scan to Add

Users can scan barcodes to add items directly to their cart with a single tap.

One-Tap Checkout

Streamlined checkout flow using Google Pay, digital wallets, and cards.

AI Chat Assistant

Built-in support for assistance, store navigation, and FAQs.

Final Thoughts

Our design philosophy turned retail complexity into human-centered simplicity. By engineering adaptive workflows and intuitive scan-to-cart dashboards, we ensured every customer experiences NoQ Store as a personal, trusted catalyst for seamless shopping.
